ABOUT ME:

Growing up I was never allowed outside as my mother was never home and whoever was taking care of me at the time would never let me outside as it was a hassle for them to watch me in that situation, so the only thing I had was my handheld game, a DSI. I played various games released by Nintendo from mega man to Pac-Man, but the game I fell in love with the most was the game released by GameFreak, Pokemon. I loved the various designs of the characters and the story, and when I was younger I always wondered how people created things like that. When I entered high school I was finally able to get access to computers and a crash course in learning JavaScript, though it was just the basics of how to code I fell in love with the process. Every adult around me always complained about how the work they were doing was something they hated but it was something they had to do, but when I was writing code I didn’t feel that way I felt focused and motivated to continue writing code. I knew that programming and development was something that I wanted to chase after as a career.
